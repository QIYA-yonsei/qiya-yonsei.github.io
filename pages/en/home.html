Qiskit Fall Festival @ Yonsei 2025

October 31 - November 1, 2025 | Yonsei University Sinchon Campus Science Hall

Register Now

The Qiskit Fall Festival @ Yonsei is an annual event at Yonsei University hosted by QIYA(Quantum Informatics at Yonsei Academy) and sponsored by IBM Quantum. Our mission is to bring students, researchers, and industry professionals together . Whether you're an expert or just starting your quantum journey, you'll find inspiring talks, hands-on workshops, and invaluable networking opportunities. Join us as we dive into the latest advancements in quantum algorithms, hardware, and software.
