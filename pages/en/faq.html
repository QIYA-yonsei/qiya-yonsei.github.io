The hackathon is offered in two difficulties for all experience levels: Beginner and Challenger.

  • Beginner Difficulty: This track is designed for those who are new to quantum computing or Qiskit. Before the hackathon, there will be an introductory session on the basics of quantum computing and Qiskit. The problems will cover the fundamentals of Qiskit, making it a great starting point for anyone, even with no prior knowledge about quantum computing. But please note that you'll have to know how to code in Python even for the beginner difficulty.
  • Challenger Difficulty: This track is for participants who have previous experience with quantum computing and are comfortable with quantum programming frameworks like Qiskit. It will feature challenging problems on more in-depth topics, providing a great opportunity to test your skills.
